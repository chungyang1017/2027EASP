About

About the conference

The East Asian Social Policy research network (EASP) brings together researchers, policymakers, and practitioners with a shared interest in social policy and welfare across East Asia. Since its founding, EASP has fostered comparative dialogue on pensions, health and long-term care, family policy, labour markets, inequality, and the changing role of the welfare state.

The 2027 conference turns to the digital age: as East Asia leads the global semiconductor and AI economy, how do automation, datafication, and platform power reshape inequality, welfare, and governance across the region?

Special focus: digital inequality and the new governance challenges of an automated, data-driven society.

Conference theme

Back to the Future

Digitalization, Automation, and the Future of Welfare Governance in East Asia

East Asia sits at the center of the AI and semiconductor revolution — yet the same digital transformation deepens digital inequality and raises urgent questions of algorithmic and data governance. As automation reshapes work, care, and the state itself, EASP 2027 asks who is left behind, who governs the algorithms, and how East Asian welfare states must be rebuilt for the digital age.

AI & Automation

Labour displacement, reskilling, and the future of work.

Digital Inequality

The divide in access, skills, and data — and the new social cleavages it creates.

Algorithmic & Data Governance

Accountability, transparency, surveillance, and digital rights.

Digital Welfare States

Algorithmic benefits, digital public services, and inclusion.

Platform & Gig Work

New social risks, precarity, and protection.

Semiconductors & the Social Contract

Tech-led growth, distribution, and who benefits in East Asia.

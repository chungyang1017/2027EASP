About

About the conference

The East Asian Social Policy research network (EASP) brings together researchers, policymakers, and practitioners with a shared interest in social policy and welfare across East Asia. Since its founding, EASP has fostered comparative dialogue on pensions, health and long-term care, family policy, labour markets, inequality, and the changing role of the welfare state.

The 2027 conference continues this tradition, welcoming scholars from across the region and around the world to share new research, build collaborations, and debate the future of social policy in East Asia.

Conference theme

Back to the Future

Digitalization, Automation, and the Future of Welfare Governance in East Asia

East Asia sits at the center of the AI and semiconductor revolution — and at the frontier of demographic and social change. As digitalization and automation reshape work, care, and the state itself, EASP 2027 asks how welfare states and welfare governance must be rebuilt for the digital age.

AI & Automation

Labour displacement, reskilling, and the future of work.

Digital Welfare States

Algorithmic governance, digital benefits, and social rights.

Semiconductors & Growth

Tech-led development and the East Asian social contract.

Platform & Gig Work

New social risks, precarity, and protection.

Care & Ageing Tech

Robotics, telecare, and demographic transition.

Data & Governance

Privacy, inclusion, surveillance, and welfare administration.
